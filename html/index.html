What is CEC About?

Curtin Engineers Club (CEC) is a multi-disciplinary engineering club that holds numerous social and professional events, helps students connect with each other and provides resources to its members. Being a social club, CEC welcomes all students, not just engineering students, with events such as BOTL or Pubcrawl purely for socialising.
CEC can also support those looking to further their career with our networking nights and skill based workshops. These are more tailored to engineering students, with professional events hosting industry representatives for you to establish relations with and learn about the workforce from.

Come find us in the Engineering Pavilion, room 106!
