Who Are We?

Why Join the Club?

Curtin Engineers Club aims to foster a community where students can relax and socialise with their peers, whilst also building industry connections with companies. Become of the 700 members and find someone in your unit, in your field or just a friend to drink with. Whilst our industry events are targeted to engineering students, our social events are fun for any student.
