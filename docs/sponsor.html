Why Sponsor Us?

With a membership base of over 700 students, Curtin Engineers Club can reach students of all engineering majors and even students from other disciplines. Whilst our social events garner great interest, our professional events allow students to extend their skills, learn about the industry and gain connections. Sponsoring us would allow your company to be one of those industry connections to our memberbase. Across the sponsorship levels there are many benefits such as; personalised events, sponsored emails and social media posts and your logo on our merchandise. Being a non-profit, our club relies on your sponsorships to create amazing events and bring people together within the engineering community.

Our Professional Events

Personalised events are a part of some of our sponsor packages, these events are ways to show off your company to students and find new talent. Previously these events have manifested in multiple ways, being; Site Tours, stalls at our BOTL or networking events and even workshops that teach skills your company specialises in.
